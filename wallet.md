## Wallet Options Considered
1. **Self-custody wallet** (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
   - Pros: Full control, no KYC
   - Cons: Security responsibility, recovery phrase management

2. **Exchange wallet** (Coinbase, Binance, etc.)
   - Pros: Easier fiat conversion, insurance
   - Cons: KYC required, platform risk

3. **Hardware wallet** (Ledger, Trezor)
   - Pros: Maximum security
   - Cons: Cost, physical device needed

## Recommended Approach
Given the mission constraints (no money spending without approval), we should:
1. Create a free self-custody wallet (MetaMask or similar)
2. Document the public address for receiving payments
3. Keep private keys/seed phrase secure (not in repo)
4. Use a testnet first for verification workflow
